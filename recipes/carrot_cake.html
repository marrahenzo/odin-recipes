Carrot Cake

Description

An easy homemade Carrot Cake recipe for a super moist cake made from scratch. This traditional carrot cake is tender and perfectly spiced, with balanced sweetness. This southern carrot cake recipe is sinfully delicious.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 cup grated carrots
  • 2 cup flour
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup walnuts (chopped, optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

  2. Prepare cake pan by greasing it with butter.

  3. In a medium bowl fitted with a mixer, combine sugar and eggs and mix on medium/low for 4-5 minutes.

  4. Add flour, baking powder and cinnamon and mix for 1-2 minutes.

  5. Add thinly shredded carrots and walnuts and stir by hand.

  6. Pour in cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes or until center of cake comes out clean when a toothpick is inserted into the center.

  7. Allow cake to cool before removing from pans.
